# Phase 3: Visual Merchandising Update

This guide outlines how to transform your listing image gallery from simple product photos into highly informative infographics that resolve customer doubts and drive traffic to your website.

---

## 📋 Action Checklist

*   [ ] **Design the Key Infographic Slides:**
    *   Create clean, high-resolution graphics using your brand design system (Quicksand font, charcoal text, soft blue highlight accents).
    *   *Slide 2 (The Solution):* Close-up photo showing the **360-degree heavy-duty elastic** or the **3-sided zipper** in action. Text overlay: *"Never slips off. Anchored for custom beds."*
    *   *Slide 3 (Fabric Guide):* A comparison matrix comparing `PremaCotton` (crisp, breathable), `EcoLuxe` (organic, natural), `BreezePlus` (cooling), and `CloudSoft` (soft, wrinkle-resistant).
    *   *Slide 4 (Size & Shape Guide):* Diagram showing V-berth shapes (for marine) or standard bunk dimensions (for RV/truck) to help customers choose.
    *   *Slide 5 (How to Order):* Simple 3-step graphic: (1) Measure your bed, (2) Select size/send message, (3) Handcrafted and shipped express.
*   [ ] **Design the DTC Website Migration Slide:**
    *   *Slide 6:* A beautifully branded graphic promoting the website:  
        `"Want it cheaper? Configure your exact dimensions live on our 3D configurator at www.mildmate.com and save 10-15% instantly! (Use code: WELCOME15)"`
*   [ ] **Update Listing Images:**
    *   Replace the outdated photo galleries on your top 10 best-selling listings with the new infographic slides.

---

## 💡 Strategy Notes
Marketplace shoppers make purchasing decisions in seconds, often looking only at photos without reading descriptions. Infographics ensure they understand the custom nature of your products, the fabrics, and the availability of direct discounts on your website.
