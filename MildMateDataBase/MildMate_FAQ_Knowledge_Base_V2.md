## 1. Custom Marine & V-Berth Bedding
**Related Products:** Custom Boat Sheets, CloudSoft Marine Bedding

### Q: I have a specific boat model (e.g., Beneteau Antares 11, Apreamare Cabinato 11, or SS7 Bayliner). Do you have templates or fitments for these?
**Engineering Solution:** We specialize in bespoke fitments for these specific hull designs [cite: 15, 51]. While we maintain a library of common shapes, we treat every cabin bed as a unique project to ensure a "Built-In" appearance [cite: 16, 75].
* **"Zero-Friction" Measurement Protocol:** No professional tape measure or complex templates are required [cite: 17, 77]. Simply place a standard credit card on the mattress for scale and send a photo; our team uses digital scaling to extract precise dimensions [cite: 18, 52, 53].
* **Blueprint Verification:** Before production, we generate a digital blueprint of your hull's geometry for your approval to confirm orientation (head vs. foot) and taper [cite: 19, 78].
* **Heavy-Motion Fitment:** Sheets are custom-tailored to the exact taper of the hull, featuring a heavy-duty 360-degree elastic perimeter to stay anchored during heavy seas or when lifting mattresses for storage access [cite: 54, 79].

## 2. Family Co-Sleeping & "Mega-Bed" Systems
**Related Products:** Family Fitted Sheet, Custom Quilted Protector, BedBridge Connector

### Q: Can you create a single fitted sheet and protector for combined beds (e.g., 290cm total width)?
**Engineering Solution:** Yes. We manufacture "Unified Surface" conversions for any combined footprint, including massive setups up to 290cm x 183cm [cite: 22, 23, 81].
* **Seamless Architecture:** For large-format setups, we use a single, 100% seamless piece of fabric to eliminate the uncomfortable center ridge found in standard joined sheets [cite: 24, 82].
* **The "Bridge-Buffer" Layer:** We recommend our quilted protector to act as a mechanical leveling layer, using its loft to flatten convex bumps and make the transition across a BedBridge imperceptible [cite: 83].
* **The "Mechanical Pinch" Anchor:** We include a 2-inch secure tuck-under beyond your pocket depth. This uses the mattresses' own weight to "pinch" the fabric in place, preventing it from sliding [cite: 25, 84].
* **Multi-Sheet Discounts:** We offer a 20% discount for customers ordering two or more custom family sheets (e.g., UK Double x2 setups) [cite: 27, 85].

## 4. Dakimakura (Body Pillow) Modular System
**Related Products:** TPU Inner Protector, BreezePlus Outer Cover, Ultimate Bundle

### Q: Why do you recommend a 2-layer system instead of a single-layer sewn TPU unit?
**Engineering Solution:** Our Professional 2-Layer System is superior for three reasons [cite: 30, 90]:
1. **Durability:** Frequent washing can cause TPU membranes to delaminate if sewn directly to outer fabric [cite: 31, 90]. Our system lets you wash the outer cover (PremaCotton or BreezePlus) while the TPU liner remains protected [cite: 32, 91].
2. **Waterproof Integrity:** Sewing TPU directly creates needle holes that can leak [cite: 33, 92]. A separate inner liner provides 100% spill-proof protection [cite: 34, 93].
3. **Acoustics & Feel:** Separate layers ensure the pillow stays soft and quiet; bonding them creates a "crinkly" or stiff feel [cite: 35, 95].

## 5. Duvet Covers & BedBridge Connector
**Related Products:** 3-Sided Zipper Duvet, BedBridge Connector

### Q: What makes the 3-sided zipper better for heavy duvets?
**Engineering Solution:** The "Suitcase-Style" load allows the top layer to flip back completely. You simply lay the heavy insert flat and zip it closed, cutting bed-making time by ~90% and eliminating heavy lifting [cite: 61].
* **BedBridge Mechanics:** This is a mechanical component sold separately from sheets [cite: 67, 68]. It requires mattresses to stay at the same height; independent adjustment of split beds will dislodge the bridge [cite: 71, 72].
