# 01_Fabric_Intelligence_Guide

## Overview
MildMate utilizes four premium fabric collections for Group 1 (Custom Bedding) and a specialized high-performance TPU membrane for Group 2 (Waterproof Protection). This guide defines the technical properties and target users for each material to ensure customers find the perfect match for their lifestyle.

---

## Group 1: Custom Bedding Fabrics

### 1. BreezePlus Collection (Advanced Cooling Cotton Blend)
* **The Science**: A high-performance fusion of 50% Long-Staple Cotton and 50% Specialized Microfiber.
* **Key Benefit**: **Temperature Regulation.** Clinically shown to be 3-5°C cooler than standard cotton. It actively draws heat away from the body rather than trapping it.
* **Additional Benefits**: 
    * **Pet-Friendly**: Specifically engineered to be resistant to pet hair.
    * **100% Chemical-Free**: Safe for skin and sustainable.
* **Best For**: 
    * Hot sleepers, night sweats, families with allergies, and tropical climates.
    * Pet owners seeking a durable, hair-resistant sleeping surface.
* **Colors**: 9 Cooling Colors — Dark Grey (#4D545B), Silver (#B7BEC8), Sand (#D9D1C1), Sky (#9CCAE1), Emerald (#618283), Sea (#5A7DA2), Pure White (#FFFFFF), Baby Pink (#E9B7BF), Ivory (#F1EFE1).

### 2. CloudSoft Collection (Premium Microfiber Excellence)
* **The Science**: Highly engineered 120 GSM double-brushed microfiber with a signature embossed pattern.
* **Key Benefit**: **Moisture Resistance & Durability.** Resists salt air and ambient humidity. It is quick-drying and requires no ironing.
* **Best For**: Marine, Yacht, and RV cabins; families with children seeking soft, stain-resistant bedding.
* **Colors**: 12 Sophisticated Colors — Mint (#67E4D3), Charcoal (#5E5E5E), Grey (#A5A9B4), Sapphire (#385E9D), Forest (#486C6A), Denim (#1E4477), RoseGold (#D48C98), Beige (#E1D1B1), Ovaltine (#B99F87), White (#FFFFFF), Lavender (#9581B1), Olive (#93B09D).

### 3. PremaCotton Collection (The Purest Natural Choice)
* **The Science**: 100% Pure Long-Staple Natural Cotton.
* **Key Benefit**: **Chemical-Free Purity.** Becomes softer with every wash and offers the traditional "crisp" feel of luxury hotel linens.
* **Certifications**: **OEKO-TEX Standard 100 Certified.** Independently tested and guaranteed free from harmful substances.
* **Best For**: Individuals with sensitive skin, infants (0-6 months), and pure cotton enthusiasts.
* **Colors**: Pristine Snow White Only.

### 4. EcoLuxe Collection (Natural Calico Comfort)
* **The Science**: Premium Calico (Greige) cotton in its natural, unbleached state. Woven in a durable plain-weave pattern that softens with every wash while maintaining its raw, earthy character.
* **Key Benefit**: **Minimal Processing — Closer to Nature.** No harsh chemical processing or bleaching.
* **Best For**: Eco-conscious consumers and parents seeking a natural sanctuary.
* **Colors**: Natural Vanilla Linen.

## Group 2: Waterproof Protection Fabrics

### 5. Performance TPU (Thermoplastic Polyurethane)
* **The Science**: A thin, flexible, microporous membrane bonded to a soft top-layer fabric.
* **Key Benefit**: **100% Waterproof but 100% Breathable.** Blocks liquid but allows body heat vapor to escape.
* **Mechanical Property**: **Silent & No-Crinkle.** Eliminates the "plastic noise" common in standard protectors.
* **Best For**: Bedwetting protection, pet-friendly utility, and extending mattress life.
