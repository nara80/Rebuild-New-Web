# MildMate Master Brand Bible & Production Standards

## 1. Visual Identity (CI)
These standards ensure every marketing asset across Etsy, eBay, Shopee, and our website feels like it belongs to the same family.

* **Primary CI Color:** #2c96f4 (Specific Vibrant Blue). This color represents our professional cooling performance and trust.
* **Typography:** The MildMate font is clean, rounded, highly readable, and approachable. It must feel modern and friendly to reduce customer "decision fatigue."
* **Photography/Image Pattern:**
    * **Context:** Images must show the product in practical, real-life use (e.g., on a boat mattress, a family bed, or being fitted by a user).
    * **Overlays:** Use benefit-driven text overlays (e.g., "Change in 60 Seconds," "Zero-Friction Measurement") using the brand typography.
    * **Consistency:** Every listing should include a "Hero" shot followed by detail shots of zippers, fabric texture, and the measurement method.
