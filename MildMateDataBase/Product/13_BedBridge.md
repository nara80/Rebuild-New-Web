The MildMate BedBridge Connector solves the disruptive "center gap" issue common in split king configurations, twin-to-king conversions, and family co-sleeping setups.

1. **Premium Microfiber Filling:** Avoids cheap memory foam that breaks down, crumbles, and traps body heat. MildMate uses high-density microfiber acting like a "firm cloud" that remains cool and structurally sound over time.
2. **Smart No-Strap System:** Eliminates clumsy under-mattress straps or structural buckles. It utilizes strategic friction and vertical T-shape anchoring combined with 360° sheet tension.
