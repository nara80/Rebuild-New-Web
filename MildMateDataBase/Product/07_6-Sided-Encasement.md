# Product Functional Instances: MildMate 6-Sided Mattress Encasement

## 1. The "Tight-Space" Installation (For RV & Van Conversions)
**Instance:** Engineered for mattresses located in confined built-in platforms, alcoves, or "bunk" configurations where access to the bottom of the mattress is restricted.
* **The Problem:** Standard 6-sided encasements often place zippers in the middle of the sidewall or on the bottom, requiring the user to fully lift and flip a heavy mattress in a cramped cabin.
* **The Functional Solution:** By placing the **zipper track along the top perimeter**, the encasement can be installed or removed while the mattress remains on the platform. This "Low-Lift" design allows the user to slide the cover over the base and zip it closed from the top, making it ideal for the limited-clearance environments of nomadic living.

## 2. Hardware-Safe Lapped Construction
**Instance:** Designed for high-end bedding setups where external zippers can snag, scratch, or damage decorative linens.
* **The Problem:** Exposed zipper teeth can catch on delicate top sheets or create friction against the bed frame.
* **The Functional Solution:** The **lapped zipper design** features a dedicated fabric flap that covers the entire hardware track. This creates a smooth, snag-free exterior surface, ensuring that the zipper remains hidden and protected from interfering with secondary bedding layers.

## 3. The 360° Mechanical Lockdown (Absolute Enclosure)
**Instance:** Ideal for long-term storage or environments where the mattress is exposed to the elements from all angles (e.g., under-bed storage access).
* **The Problem:** Standard "fitted" protectors only cover 5 sides, leaving the bottom of the mattress vulnerable to dust, debris, and movement-related wear against the bed base.
* **The Functional Solution:** The **6-sided architectural design** provides a complete mechanical seal. Once zipped, the mattress is entirely contained within a single structural shell, ensuring that the base, sidewalls, and top surface are equally shielded from any environmental contact.

## 4. Production-Optimized Top/Bottom Placement
**Instance:** Tailored for rapid deployment in custom-sized commercial or hospitality settings.
* **The Problem:** Complex zipper paths increase the time required for assembly and maintenance.
* **The Functional Solution:** The placement of the zipper on the **top or bottom face** (rather than the sidewall) streamlines the manufacturing process for custom dimensions. This orientation allows for a more efficient "envelope" style closure that maintains structural integrity even on non-standard, thin, or ultra-deep mattress profiles.

## 5. Bespoke Volume Alignment (For Odd-Shaped Bunks)
**Instance:** Specifically built for "Short Queens," RV Bunks, and custom-cut foam mattresses found in antique beds or marine vessels.
* **The Problem:** Off-the-shelf encasements are built for standard heights (10-12"). On a thin 4-inch van mattress or a 15-inch luxury hybrid, the excess fabric bunches up or the cover becomes impossible to close.
* **The Functional Solution:** Every encasement is **hand-crafted to precise volumetric dimensions**. By matching the exact height, width, and length of the mattress, the encasement maintains a drum-tight fit that prevents "fabric migration," ensuring the surface remains flat and stable during sleep.
