## The Value Proposition: "The Hybrid Zero-Friction Method"
Traditional boat mattress measurement is difficult and error-prone. MildMate eliminates this friction by doing the heavy lifting before the customer begins.

- **Research-First Strategy:** MildMate identifies the boat model's standard engineering dimensions first. Instead of asking the customer to measure from zero, we ask them to "verify" our data.
- **Dual-Verification Choice:** - **Tape Measure:** Traditional centimeter-to-centimeter verification against the blueprint.

## The 7-Step Workflow

### 1. Model Research & Shape Identification
Upon customer inquiry (e.g., "Beneteau Antares 11 2023"), MildMate researches the boat's hull geometry to determine the specific mattress code (A-I) and standard engineering dimensions.

### 2. Pre-Filled Template Delivery
MildMate sends a digital blueprint through Etsy Chat that is **already populated** with suggested dimensions (Head, Foot, Length, and Depth). This transforms a "measuring task" into a "verification task."

### 3. Verification (Tape or Card)
The customer confirms the pre-filled dimensions using their preferred method:
- **Tape Measure:** Checking physical CM measurements against the template numbers.
- **Credit Card Method:** Flipping any standard card (exactly 8.56 cm long) to provide "card counts" for each side.

### 4. Submission of Refinements
The customer confirms if the standard dimensions match or provides minor adjustments based on specific dealer customizations (e.g., "Side C is 2 cm longer").

### 5. Precision Digital Drafting
MildMate refines the blueprint using **Vibrant Blue (#2c96f4)** technical markings. The final schematic includes the boat model name and the MildMate logo.

### 6. Final Quote & Approval
The customer receives the finalized, professional blueprint for visual confirmation alongside a "down-to-the-penny" price quote for their full bundle (Sheets, Topper, Duvet, etc.).

### 7. Physical Order Conversion
Upon approval, the customer receives a **$10 OFF promo code** to purchase their physical **CloudSoft** marine bedding, fulfilling the credit promise.
