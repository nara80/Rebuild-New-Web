# Product Functional Instances: MildMate Custom Extra Deep Pocket Fitted Sheet

## 1. The "High-Profile" Anchor (For Pillow-Top & Luxury Mattresses)
**Instance:** Engineered for ultra-thick luxury mattresses that standard retail sheets cannot fully cover.
* **The Problem:** Standard "deep" sheets (12-14") often pop off the corners of modern pillow-top or hybrid mattresses during the night.
* **The Functional Solution:** With a **true 20-inch depth** (customizable up to 24"+), this sheet provides full sidewall coverage. The generous depth ensures the corners stay locked firmly under the mattress base, eliminating the need for aftermarket sheet straps or clips.

## 2. The "Topper-Integrated" Fit (For Memory Foam & Gel Overlays)
**Instance:** Designed for users who have added thick 3-inch or 4-inch memory foam toppers to their existing mattress.
* **The Problem:** Adding a topper increases the total height of the sleep surface, causing regular sheets to slide up and expose the mattress sides.
* **The Functional Solution:** The **custom-sized pocket depth** accounts for the combined height of the mattress and the topper. The **360° heavy-duty elastic** creates a unified fit that holds both the topper and the mattress together as a single unit, preventing the topper from shifting independently.

## 3. The "Zero-Slip" Perimeter (For Active Sleepers)
**Instance:** Optimized for individuals who move frequently during sleep or use adjustable bed bases.
* **The Problem:** Sheets with elastic only at the corners tend to bunch up in the center or "smile" (pull up) along the sides when the bed is articulated or when a sleeper turns.
* **The Functional Solution:** Featuring **reinforced elastic around the entire 360° perimeter**, the sheet maintains constant tension on all four sides. This "Perfect Lock" system ensures the fabric remains taut across the sleeping surface, regardless of movement or bed position.

## 4. The "Secure Tuck-Under" (For Enhanced Grip)
**Instance:** Specifically built for mattresses with smooth or slippery ticking materials.
* **The Problem:** Even deep sheets can slide upward if there isn't enough fabric gripped underneath the weight of the mattress.
* **The Functional Solution:** Every sheet is designed with a **2-inch secure tuck-under** beyond the specified pocket depth. This additional fabric allowance uses the weight of the mattress itself to "pinch" the sheet in place, providing a mechanical advantage against slipping.

## 5. The "Niche-Dimension" Solution (For RV, Marine & Antique Beds)
**Instance:** Tailored for non-standard sleeping platforms found in yachts, RVs, or heirloom antique frames.
* **The Problem:** Mass-produced sheets are only available in standard Home-Twin, Queen, or King sizes, leaving owners of custom-shaped mattresses with ill-fitting bedding.
* **The Functional Solution:** As a custom manufacturer, MildMate provides **bespoke dimensions** for any height, width, or length. This ensures a precision fit for clipped-corner RV mattresses or thin but wide marine berths, providing the same structural integrity as a standard household bed.
