## 3. The "Zero-Friction" Measurement Method (Credit Card Scaling)
Measuring custom boat beds with tape measures is prone to error. MildMate uses a global standard to ensure 100% accuracy.

* **The Scale:** Every standard credit card/ID card worldwide is exactly **85.60 mm (3.375 in)** long. [cite: 02_Marine_and_RV_Measurement_CreditCard.md, 005-Measure-01.jpg]
* **The Process:**
    1.  **Grab a Card:** Any standard credit card or ID.
    2.  **Flip End-to-End:** Place the card at the edge and "flip" it along the length and width of the mattress.
    3.  **Count & Submit:** Customer submits the count (e.g., "S1 is 20 cards and half") via Etsy Chat. [cite: 005-Measure-01.jpg, 005-Measure-03.jpg]
* **Digital Blueprint:** MildMate converts card counts into a digital schematic for customer approval before sewing. [cite: 02_Marine_and_RV_Measurement_CreditCard.md, 005-Measure-03.jpg]

## 5. Technical Manufacturing (The Golden Formula)
Used for calculating the physical cutting lines for the production team.

* **Variables:**
    * $W_{top}$: Top Width (Head)
    * $W_{bottom}$: Bottom Width (Foot)
    * $L$: Total Length
    * $D$: Mattress Depth (Thickness)
* **The Pocket Notch ($N$):** $N = D + 3"$ (Includes 2" tuck + 1" sewing allowance). [cite: MildMate_V-Berth_Knowledge_Base_v5.md]
* **Cutting Dimensions:**
    * Fabric Top Width: $W_{top} + (2 	imes N)$
    * Fabric Bottom Width: $W_{bottom} + (2 	imes N)$
* **Corner Construction:** $N 	imes N$ square notches are cut from all four corners and sewn vertically to create the 3D pocket. [cite: MildMate_V-Berth_Knowledge_Base_v5.md]
