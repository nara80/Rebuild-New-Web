# Knowledge Base: MildMate Precision Pre-Order Service

## Service Overview
**Listing Title:** Custom Boat Mattress Measurement Blueprint | Digital Pattern & Exact Quote for V Berth Bedding

The **MildMate Precision Pre-Order Service** is a $10 digital drafting and consultation service designed to eliminate measurement errors for custom marine bedding. It provides customers with a professional digital blueprint and an exact price quote before they commit to a physical order.

---

## The Value Proposition: "The Zero-Friction Method"
Measuring odd-shaped boat berths (V-Berths, French beds, etc.) is traditionally difficult. MildMate solves this by using a global standard: **The Credit Card.**
- **Standardized Measurement:** Every credit card is 3.375 inches (8.56 cm) long.
- **Accuracy:** Allows for a "1% Tolerance" for a perfect cinch-fit.
- **Incentive:** The $10 service fee is 100% credited back to the customer via a promo code for their final **CloudSoft** sheet order.

---

## The 7-Step Workflow

### 1. Shape Selection
The customer identifies their bed shape from the **MildMate Shape Options Table**:
- **V-Berths:** Narrow Foot (Code A), Wide-Foot (Code B), King Size (Code C).
- **Round Ends:** Single Round End (Code D), King Aft Round End (Code E).
- **Complex Shapes:** Quarter Berth (Code F), Cut-Off Corner (Code G), Octagonal Bunk (Code H), Custom Angle (Code I).

### 2. Custom Template Delivery
Upon purchase, MildMate sends a customized draft template through Etsy Chat. This template features clearly numbered sides corresponding to the chosen geometry.

### 3. Measurement (No Tape Measure Required)
Customers flip any standard credit card end-to-end along the edges of the mattress.

### 4. Card Count Submission
Customers message their counts (e.g., "Side 1: 10.5 cards, Side 2: 8 cards") directly in the chat.

### 5. Digital Drafting
The MildMate team converts card counts into precise physical dimensions (inches and centimeters) to create a final geometric blueprint.

### 6. Review & Approval
The blueprint is sent back to the customer for visual confirmation and a final, "down-to-the-penny" price quote.

### 7. Conversion to Physical Order
Once approved, the customer receives a $10 OFF promo code to purchase their physical **CloudSoft MildMate Marine Fitted Sheets**.
