# Phase 4: Launching Sleeper Cab Niche

This guide covers the launch of the new **RV & Truck Fitted Sheet** on Etsy and eBay to capture the highly lucrative US and Australian semi-truck sleeper cab market.

---

## 📋 Action Checklist

*   [ ] **Create the Etsy Listing:**
    *   **Title:** `Custom Semi-Truck Sleeper Cab Fitted Sheets - Fits Freightliner, Kenworth, Peterbilt, Volvo Cabs | Breathable & Durable Bedding`
    *   **Variation 1 (Sizes):**
        *   `32" x 80" (Freightliner Cascadia/Coronado)`
        *   `35" x 79" (International/Mack)`
        *   `38" x 80" (Volvo VNL/Kenworth T680)`
        *   `42" x 80" (Extra Wide / Custom Sleeper)`
        *   `Custom Size (Message dimensions)`
    *   **Variation 2 (Fabric & Color):** Select your most breathable options (`BreezePlus`, `PremaCotton`) and dirt-hiding colors (Charcoal Grey, Navy Blue, Chocolate Brown).
*   [ ] **Create the eBay Hero Listing:**
    *   **Title:** `Custom Fitted Sheets for Semi Truck Sleeper Cab - Fits Kenworth Peterbilt Freightliner Volvo | Custom Any Size`
    *   Add key search terms in the description: `semi truck bedding`, `sleeper cab sheets`, `Peterbilt bunk sheets`, `Kenworth mattress protector`.
*   [ ] **Design Bunk-Specific Infographic Slides:**
    *   Create a graphic showing how home-use Twin sheets slide off truck bunks, explaining how MildMate's **360-degree elastic system** keeps sheets anchored during transit.
*   [ ] **Monitor Traffic and Search Queries:**
    *   In the Etsy Shop Manager, monitor which search terms drive drivers to your listing. Adjust tags and titles based on this data.

---

## 💡 Strategy Notes
Truck drivers spend weeks living in their sleeper cabs and value high-quality rest, but they struggle to find sheets that stay on their custom, non-standard mattresses. Emphasizing fitment for US/AU truck brands (Freightliner, Kenworth, Volvo, Peterbilt) will immediately establish credibility.
