# MildMate Etsy Performance Summary
## January–June 2026

## Executive Snapshot

- **4,938 visits** — up **83%** versus the same period last year.
- **21 orders** generated **USD 3,777.35** in revenue.
- Reported conversion rate: **0.4%**.
- Average order value: approximately **USD 180**.
- Listings received **7,383 views**, averaging **1.5 listing views per visit**.

> **Bottom line:** Traffic growth is strong, and customers clearly respond to MildMate’s specialist custom-bedding products. The biggest opportunity is improving conversion from visits and abandoned carts into completed orders.

## Main Concerns

### 1. Conversion is too low for the amount of traffic

A **0.4% conversion rate** means many shoppers are browsing without purchasing. With an estimated average order value near **USD 180**, even a modest conversion improvement could materially increase revenue.

### 2. Abandoned-cart value is significant

- **18 abandoned carts**.
- Combined cart value: approximately **USD 2,473**.
- Only **5 shoppers** received cart offers.

This abandoned value equals roughly **65% of completed revenue**, making cart recovery the fastest near-term opportunity.

### 3. Etsy Search declined

- Etsy Search delivered only **125 visits**, down **39%**.

Traffic is growing mainly through Etsy recommendations, Etsy marketing, and other discovery surfaces—not direct keyword search. This creates risk if Etsy stops recommending the listings as strongly.

### 4. Many listings attract views but produce no orders

Examples include:

- Custom family bed flat sheet — **287 views, 0 orders**.
- EcoLuxe 3-sided zipper duvet cover — **193 views, 0 orders**.
- Custom family fitted sheet — **669 views, 0 orders**.
- RV and van mattress encasement — **400 views, 0 orders**.

These listings may have issues with the first image, offer clarity, price confidence, measurement process, shipping expectations, or customer trust.

---

## Recommended Priorities

### Priority 1 — Recover abandoned carts

- Confirm that abandoned-cart offers are enabled for every eligible shopper.
- Test a modest incentive that protects margin.
- Add stronger urgency and reassurance in listing copy: easy measurement help, made-to-order timing, and direct support before purchase.

### Priority 2 — Build around the proven winners

Create more focused variations and listing images for:

- Marine and boat fitted sheets.
- 3-sided zipper duvet covers.
- Oversized and co-sleep family bedding.
- BedBridge and joined-mattress solutions.

Do not spread effort evenly across all listings. Concentrate first on categories already producing orders.

### Priority 3 — Improve weak high-traffic listings

For listings with high views but no sales:

- Replace the first image with a clearer problem–solution visual.
- Show exact use cases immediately: family bed, RV, boat, adjustable bed, or custom shape.
- Simplify size and measurement instructions.
- Explain what happens after the buyer orders.
- Add one strong trust block covering materials, production time, customisation, and support.

### Priority 4 — Rebuild Etsy Search visibility

- Rewrite titles around precise buyer intent rather than broad product names.
- Strengthen the first words of titles with high-value use cases.
- Align titles, tags, attributes, images, and descriptions around the same search intent.
- Separate very different use cases into dedicated listings instead of combining too many audiences in one listing.

### Priority 5 — Use social media to feed proven Etsy listings

Social traffic rose **633%**. Direct short-form content toward the listings already converting:

- Boat mattress fitting problems.
- Family-bed gaps and oversized sheets.
- Difficult duvet insertion versus the 3-sided zipper solution.
- Mattress-connector demonstrations.

---

## Practical 30-Day Action Plan

1. **Optimise the top five revenue-producing listings first.**
2. **Audit the four highest-view listings with zero orders.**
3. **Activate or improve abandoned-cart offers.**
4. **Create stronger first images and short videos for marine, family-bed, and zipper-duvet listings.**
5. **Rewrite search titles and tags for the listings losing Etsy Search traffic.**
6. **Track conversion by listing every week—not only total shop traffic.**

---

## Management Takeaway

MildMate has achieved excellent traffic growth and has proven demand for high-value custom bedding. The shop does not currently have a traffic problem; it has a **conversion and listing-focus problem**.

The best commercial move is to concentrate on the specialist categories already generating orders, improve clarity and trust on high-traffic listings, and recover more of the **USD 2,473** currently sitting in abandoned carts.

> **Recommended focus:** Fewer priorities, stronger listing presentation, clearer measurement guidance, and aggressive follow-up on high-intent shoppers.
