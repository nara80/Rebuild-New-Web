## 🛠️ Step-by-Step: Consolidating 4 Fabrics & Custom Colors (Etsy Solution)

### The Problem:
1. Etsy allows a **maximum of 2 variations** (dropdowns) per listing. You cannot have dropdowns for **Fabric**, **Color**, and **Size** at the same time.
2. Each fabric collection has different colors (e.g., `EcoLuxe` is only organic natural white/cream, while `CloudSoft` has multiple dyed colors).
3. Combining all size, fabric, and color variations as separate dropdown options would exceed Etsy's **400 variation limit** (e.g., 5 sizes * 4 fabrics * 15 colors = 300+ combinations, which easily breaks if you add more sizes).

### The Solution: The "Size + Fabric + Personalization" Method
By setting **Size** and **Fabric** as your dropdown variations and using Etsy's **Personalization Text Field** for color input, you bypass all variations limits. This keeps your dropdown options at a clean 20 combinations (5 sizes * 4 fabrics) while allowing unlimited colors.

---

### Step 1: Set Up the Etsy Variations & Personalization

1. Go to your Etsy listing editor.
2. Scroll to **Variations** and click **Add variations**.
3. Create **Variation 1 (Size)**:
   * Select `Size` as the variation type.
   * Add options:
     * `Twin (68" x 86")`
     * `Full (86" x 86")`
     * `Queen (90" x 90")`
     * `King (104" x 90")`
     * `Custom Size (See Details)`
4. Create **Variation 2 (Fabric Type)**:
   * Select `Fabric` or create a custom variation named `Fabric Collection`.
   * Add options:
     * `PremaCotton (100% Cotton)`
     * `BreezePlus (Cooling)`
     * `EcoLuxe (Organic Cotton)`
     * `CloudSoft (Microfiber)`
5. Enable **Personalization**:
   * Toggle the Personalization switch to **ON**.
   * Under **Instructions for buyers**, enter:  
     `"Please refer to our color swatch image slides. Type your desired color matching your fabric choice (e.g., 'Charcoal' for BreezePlus, or 'Natural White' for EcoLuxe)."`
   * Set character limit to `100` and make it **Required**.

### Step 2: Structure the Listing Images (Color Swatch Cards)

Since customers will type their color in a text box, your listing gallery images must clearly show which colors are available for each fabric.
*   **Slide 1 (Hero):** Beautiful, styled photo of the 3-sided zipper duvet cover in a premium room.
*   **Slide 2 (Feature):** Close-up of the 3-sided zipper mechanism.
*   **Slide 3 (Fabric Chart):** Comparison matrix of the 4 fabrics.
*   **Slide 4 (PremaCotton Colors):** Color swatches labeled *"PremaCotton 100% Cotton Colors: Snow White, Charcoal, Navy..."*
*   **Slide 5 (BreezePlus Colors):** Color swatches labeled *"BreezePlus Cooling Cotton Colors: Gray, Blue, White..."*
*   **Slide 6 (EcoLuxe Colors):** Color swatches labeled *"EcoLuxe Organic Colors: Natural Cream, Soft Ivory (Chemical-Free)"*
*   **Slide 7 (CloudSoft Colors):** Color swatches labeled *"CloudSoft Microfiber Colors: White, Charcoal, Pink..."*
*   **Slide 8 (Custom Website Slide):** Graphic directing users to `www.mildmate.com` for direct custom sizes and colors.
