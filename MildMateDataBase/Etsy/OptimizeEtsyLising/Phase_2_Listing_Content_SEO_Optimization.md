# Phase 2: Listing Content & SEO Optimization

This guide covers updating your listing descriptions, titles, and tags to maximize search visibility and funnel buyers to your new DTC website for custom sizes.

---

## 📋 Action Checklist

*   [ ] **Place DTC Link at the Top of Descriptions:**
    *   Insert this direct link at the very first line of every Etsy/eBay description:  
        `"👉 Need custom dimensions, specific colors, or a discount? Configure your bedding live and save 10-15% at our official site: www.mildmate.com (Use coupon code: MATE10)"`
*   [ ] **Update Titles for Custom Search Intent:**
    *   Optimize your titles to lead with keywords that highlight customization, as standard sizes are highly competitive.
    *   *Example (Marine):* `Custom Marine Fitted Sheet for V-Berth & Yacht Mattresses | Custom Any Size & Shape`
    *   *Example (Family):* `Custom Family Bed Fitted Sheet - Giant Co-Sleeping Bedding | Custom Sizes up to 10ft`
*   [ ] **Audit and Update Listing Tags:**
    *   Ensure each listing uses all 13 tags on Etsy. Remove generic tags and focus on high-intent search terms:
        *   *Marine tags:* `boat sheets`, `yacht bedding`, `v-berth sheets`, `custom boat mattress`, `marine protector`.
        *   *Family tags:* `family bed sheet`, `co sleeping sheets`, `giant sheets`, `oversized bedding`, `custom size sheets`.
        *   *RV tags:* `rv fitted sheet`, `camper bedding`, `short queen sheet`, `rv bunk sheets`, `custom rv sheet`.

---

## 💡 Strategy Notes
Etsy truncates descriptions on desktop and mobile. Placing your direct store URL at the very top (above the fold) ensures shoppers see it immediately without having to click "Read More".
