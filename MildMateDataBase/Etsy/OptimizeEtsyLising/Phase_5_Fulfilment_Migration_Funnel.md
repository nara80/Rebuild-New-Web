# Phase 5: Fulfilment & Migration Funnel

This guide covers setting up your pricing markups to offset marketplace fees, and launching the physical package insert card funnel to migrate buyers from marketplaces to your direct website.

---

## 📋 Action Checklist

*   [ ] **Apply Marketplace Pricing Markups:**
    *   Audit your Etsy and eBay prices. Set them **15% – 20% higher** than direct prices on `www.mildmate.com`.
    *   This covers the high marketplace commission fees (up to 25% for Offsite Ads) and creates a natural price incentive for customers to buy direct on their next order.
*   [ ] **Design the Package Insert Card:**
    *   Create a clean, premium double-sided card to insert into all shipped boxes.
    *   **Front side:** A warm thank-you message:  
        `"Thank you for supporting MildMate! Your custom bedding was handcrafted to order."`
    *   **Back side:** The DTC migration offer:  
        `"Need another set? Or have another custom bed (RV, Yacht, Dorm, Truck)?  
        Order directly from our new website for:  
        ✓ 10-15% lower everyday pricing  
        ✓ Live 3D size & shape configurator  
        ✓ Complete fabric catalog & custom shapes  
        Go to www.mildmate.com and use code: MATE15 for 15% off your next order."`
*   [ ] **Print the Insert Cards:**
    *   Print the cards locally on high-quality cardstock to maintain a premium brand feel.
*   [ ] **Establish packing routine:**
    *   Ensure a package insert card is placed on top of the folded bedding in every Etsy and eBay box shipped.

---

## 💡 Strategy Notes
The easiest customer to acquire is one who has already bought from you. Because they have already received your product and verified your quality, offering them a 10-15% discount + live sizing tool on your website turns them into a high-margin direct customer for all future purchases.
