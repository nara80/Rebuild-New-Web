## 2. Strategic Verification: Why Marketplaces First?

Your strategy to **optimize Etsy and eBay listings before scaling Google Ads or B2B** is **100% correct**.

*   **Existing Ad Feed Efficiency:** You are already paying for **Etsy Offsite Ads** and **eBay Promoted Listings**. Both programs syndicate your listings to Google Shopping. Optimizing these listings directly improves your Google visibility without the risk of managing direct Google Ads budgets.
*   **Built-in Trust:** Custom-made luxury bedding is a high-consideration purchase. Customers are more comfortable making their first purchase or inquiry on a trusted marketplace.
*   **Marketplace as a Lead Gen Engine:** Instead of viewing Etsy/eBay as standalone stores, treat them as **high-intent lead-generation funnels** that capture traffic and redirect complex custom orders to the live website configurator.

## 4. Marketplace Pricing & DTC Funneling

### The Constraint:
Marketplace transaction fees and Offsite Ads cost **20% to 25%** of the sale price. Manual pricing on Etsy/eBay is slow and difficult to maintain.

### The Strategy (Marketplace Premium Pricing):
1.  **Set a 15% – 20% Marketplace Markup:**
    *   Price your items higher on Etsy and eBay than direct prices on `www.mildmate.com`. 
    *   *Strategic Benefit:* Setting custom size variation prices high (e.g. King-tier price) protects margins and naturally leads buyers needing exact dimensions to explore direct options.
2.  **Implement Broad Pricing Tiers:**
    *   Group sizes into general pricing brackets (e.g., Twin/Dorm = Tier 1, Full/Queen = Tier 2, King/Cal King = Tier 3).
    *   Set the **"Custom Size"** variation price to match the highest size tier (e.g., King size pricing). In the description, note:  
        `"For custom sizes, select 'Custom Size' and enter your dimensions in the personalization box, or visit our website www.mildmate.com to use our custom sizing calculators."`

## 6. RV & Truck Sleeper Cab Fitted Sheets Strategy

### The Market Opportunity:
Semi-truck sleeper cab mattresses in the **US and Australia** represent a highly lucrative, under-served market. Long-haul truck drivers spend weeks on the road and demand high-quality sleep, but sleeper cab mattress sizes are highly non-standard (e.g., 32"x80", 35"x79", 38"x80", 42"x80", or layouts with tapered corners). Local custom bedding options in the US/AU are extremely expensive.

### The Strategy:
1.  **Position as "Premium Driver Comfort" on Etsy:**
    *   **Listing Title:** `"Custom Semi-Truck Sleeper Cab Fitted Sheets - Fits Freightliner, Kenworth, Peterbilt, Volvo Cabs | Breathable & Durable Bedding"`
    *   **Variation 1: Truck Model / Size Bracket** (Keep options under the 20-character limit):
        *   `32"x80" (Freightlnr)` (19 chars)
        *   `35"x79" (Int'l/Mack)` (19 chars)
        *   `38"x80" (Volvo/KW)` (17 chars)
        *   `42"x80" (Extra Wide)` (19 chars)
        *   `Custom Size` (11 chars)
    *   **Variation 2: Fabric & Color:** List your most breathable/cooling options (like *BreezePlus* and *PremaCotton*) and use the personalization box for color selections.
2.  **Establish a Hero Listing on eBay:**
    *   **Listing Title:** `"Custom Fitted Sheets for Semi Truck Sleeper Cab - Fits Kenworth Peterbilt Freightliner Volvo | Custom Any Size"`
    *   Target keywords: "semi truck bed sheets", "sleeper cab sheets", "Freightliner mattress sheets", "Kenworth bunk bedding".
3.  **Visual Gallery Callouts:**
    *   Use a graphic showing why home-use Twin sheets slide off semi bunks, emphasizing your **360-degree heavy-duty elastic** that anchors firmly in moving cabs.
    *   Include a callout card redirecting buyers to `www.mildmate.com/rv-truck` (or direct path `/product/rv-truck-fitted-sheet/`) for custom sizes and lower direct pricing.
