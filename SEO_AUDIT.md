# Homepage SEO & Copy Audit — MildMate Bedding

This audit uses performance insights from MildMate's Etsy shop data (Jan–April 2026) and customer sentiment analysis to optimize homepage meta tags (Titles and Descriptions) for both the English (`/`) and Thai (`/th/`) homepages.

---

## 📊 1. Business Insights & Search Intent Mapping

Based on the **MildMate Web Knowledge Base**:
* **High Average Order Value (AOV):** ~$195. Our audience is looking for high-quality, durable solutions, not bargain sheets. Our copy must feel premium.
* **Core Conversion Friction (0.4% CR):** Sizing/measurement anxiety. Our meta descriptions should reassure them that we specialize in custom shapes/measurements to reduce friction right from the search page.
* **Top Revenue Drivers (The Niches):**
  1. **Marine & Yacht Collection:** (V-Berths, aft cabins, custom boat shapes)
  2. **Family & Co-Sleeping Bedding:** (Mega beds up to 144", BedBridge connectors)
  3. **"Easy-Change" Duvet Covers:** (Signature 3-Sided Zipper suitcase system)
  4. **Specialized Protection:** (Waterproof barriers for RVs, vans, and pets)
* **Key Customer Selling Points:** "3-sided zipper" convenience and OEKO-TEX chemical-safe purity for families.

---

## ⚙️ 2. Length Constraints for SERP Display

To prevent search engine results pages (SERPs) from truncating (cutting off) our metadata, we aim for:
- **Title Tags:** 50–60 characters (max 600 pixels).
- **Meta Descriptions:** 120–160 characters (max 960 pixels).

---

## 📝 3. Optimized Copy Recommendations

Below are three strategic options. **Option A** is the recommended route because it directly bridges high-intent organic keywords with MildMate's unique selling points (3-sided zipper & custom sizes).

---

### Option A: The "Niche Dominance" Strategy (Recommended 🏆)
*Focuses on high-margin niches (Yachts, RVs, Family Beds) and calls out the unique 3-Sided Zipper and custom sizing to drive click-throughs from search engines.*

#### 🇬🇧 English Homepage (`/`)
* **Proposed Title Tag** *(58 characters)*:
  `Custom Bedding for Yachts, RVs & Family Beds | MildMate`
  - *Why it works:* Replaces the generic "Marine" with "Yachts" (higher Etsy commercial intent) and stays under the 60-character limit to avoid truncation.
* **Proposed Meta Description** *(156 characters)*:
  `Get perfect-fit custom sheets, protectors & 3-sided zipper duvet covers for yachts, RVs & family beds. OEKO-TEX certified premium cotton. Order custom size!`
  - *Why it works:* Mentions "3-sided zipper", "yachts/RVs/family beds", "OEKO-TEX certified" (safety), and addresses custom sizing fear with "Order custom size!".

#### 🇹🇭 Thai Homepage (`/th/`)
* **Proposed Title Tag** *(62 characters)*:
  `ผ้าปูที่นอนสั่งตัดตามขนาด เรือยอชท์ RV และเตียงครอบครัว | MildMate`
  - *Why it works:* Integrates "สั่งตัดตามขนาด" (custom sizes) with high-intent niches in Thai.
* **Proposed Meta Description** *(155 characters)*:
  `เครื่องนอนสั่งตัดตามขนาดสำหรับเรือยอชท์ รถบ้าน RV และเตียงครอบครัว ตัดเย็บประณีตจากผ้าเกรดพรีเมียม ปลอดสารเคมีอันตราย สั่งทำขนาดพิเศษของคุณวันนี้!`
  - *Why it works:* Highlights "สั่งตัดตามขนาด" to reduce sizing friction, mentions safety ("ปลอดสารเคมีอันตราย"), and ends with a strong CTA.
