RV & Truck Cab Bedding — Custom Fit for Life on the Road

RV mattresses, truck cab bunks, campervan conversions, and Sprinter van setups — none of these use standard home mattress sizes. They're narrower, shorter, oddly shaped, or non-rectangular. MildMate specializes in custom sizing for every vehicle sleeping environment.

CloudSoft microfiber is the recommended fabric for vehicle environments: it handles moisture from condensation, resists salt air for coastal routes, and dries quickly when you're on the move. For pet travelers, BreezePlus is also available.

  • Custom-cut to any non-standard vehicle mattress dimension
  • CloudSoft microfiber — moisture resistant, quick-drying, salt-air tolerant
  • 6-sided encasement option for complete dust and allergen protection
  • V-Berth cuts available for boat-RVs and marine-RVs combinations
  • Machine washable — built for travel-life wear and tear
  • Accepts measurements in cm or inches — any unit works
