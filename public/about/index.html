The 2019 Problem

Solving the "Mattress Gap" Paradox.

MildMate was founded in 2019 to solve a geometric failure: the "lumpy" transition of joined mattresses in co-sleeping environments. Standard retail bedding could not maintain the structural tension required for a unified surface.

We transitioned from a family home in Thailand to a specialized engineering lab by treating every bed not as furniture, but as a technical asset. We replaced DIY "guessing" with a rigorous measurement protocol.

From Founding Protocol to Global Precision.

Seven years of compounding engineering discipline.

The Founding Protocol
