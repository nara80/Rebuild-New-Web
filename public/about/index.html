The Zero-Friction Protocol.

Measuring complex, asymmetrical mattresses (V-Berths, RVs, Clipped Corners) is prone to human error. Our proprietary "Photo-Measurement" system uses a standard credit card as a universal scale reference. We convert your photos into digital blueprints with a 1-2cm tolerance before a single stitch is made.

1

Photo

Snap a photo of your mattress with a credit card placed on top. The 85.60mm card acts as a universal scale reference.

2

Digital Blueprint

Our engineers convert your photo into a parametric CAD model, adjusting for seam allowance and fabric stretch.

3

Precision Fit

Cut, sewn, and validated against the blueprint. Final tolerance: ±1cm. Dispatched within 3–5 business days.
