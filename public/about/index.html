Our Story

Crafting Comfort, Delivering Dreams — from a family's simple need to a global commitment to perfect sleep.

It All Started with a Simple Problem

As our family grew, so did our sleeping arrangements. We combined mattresses to create a larger co-sleeping space, but we quickly discovered that standard bedding just wouldn't fit. We were left with a lumpy, uncomfortable mess of sheets that never stayed in place.

Frustrated but determined, we set out to create our own solution. That personal quest for a perfect night's sleep became the spark for MildMate. Founded in 2019 from our family home in Thailand, we began with a mission to solve the bedding challenges that so many people face.

We knew that if we were struggling with this, others must be too. From that humble beginning, we've grown into a global brand, but our core values remain the same: to provide innovative, high-quality, and perfectly fitting bedding for every home.

"Crafting Comfort, Delivering Dreams"

Your Perfect Fit, Faster. At MildMate, we believe that true comfort comes from a perfect fit. That's why we specialize in custom-made bedding, tailored to your unique needs.

Crafted with Care in Thailand

Every MildMate product is crafted with care by our team of skilled artisans in Thailand. We believe in the power of craftsmanship and the importance of attention to detail. From the reinforced stitching to the final inspection, we take pride in creating products that are built to last.

By keeping our production in-house, we can ensure the highest standards of quality and provide our employees with a safe and supportive work environment.
