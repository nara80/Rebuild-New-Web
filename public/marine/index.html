Marine Bedding — Built for the Sea

Offshore conditions demand bedding that handles salt air, humidity, and motion. MildMate's marine collection uses CloudSoft microfiber — quick-drying, moisture-resistant, and salt-air tolerant. Every piece is custom-cut to your exact cabin dimensions.

V-Berth sheets are cut in a trapezoidal shape to match the tapered hull. No bunching, no gaps, no readjusting every morning.

  • Trapezoidal V-Berth cuts — no bunching, no gaps
  • CloudSoft microfiber — moisture and salt-air resistant
  • Custom-cut to your exact W × L × D (cm or inches)
  • 6-sided encasement option for full mattress protection
  • Machine washable, quick dry at dock
  • Available for cabin beds, aft cabins, and custom yacht shapes
