Custom Marine & V-Berth Bedding

I have a specific boat model (e.g., Beneteau Antares 11, Apreamare Cabinato 11, or SS7 Bayliner). Do you have templates or fitments for these?

We specialize in bespoke fitments for these specific hull designs. While we maintain a library of common shapes, we treat every cabin bed as a unique project to ensure a "Built-In" appearance.

  • Blueprint Verification: Before production, we generate a digital blueprint of your hull's geometry for your approval to confirm orientation (head vs. foot) and taper.
  • Heavy-Motion Fitment: Sheets are custom-tailored to the exact taper of the hull, featuring a heavy-duty 360-degree elastic perimeter to stay anchored during heavy seas or when lifting mattresses for storage access.

Family Co-Sleeping & "Mega-Bed" Systems

Can you create a single fitted sheet and protector for combined beds (e.g., 290 cm total width)?

Yes. We manufacture "Unified Surface" conversions for any combined footprint, including massive setups up to 290 cm x 183 cm.

  • Seamless Architecture: For large-format setups, we use a single, 100% seamless piece of fabric to eliminate the uncomfortable center ridge found in standard joined sheets.
  • The Bridge-Buffer Layer: We recommend our quilted protector to act as a mechanical leveling layer, using its loft to flatten convex bumps and make the transition across a BedBridge imperceptible.
  • The Mechanical Pinch Anchor: We include a 2-inch secure tuck-under beyond your pocket depth. This uses the mattresses' own weight to "pinch" the fabric in place, preventing it from sliding.
  • Multi-Sheet Discounts: We offer a 20% discount for customers ordering two or more custom family sheets.

Specialized Waterproof Protection

Can you make custom waterproof protectors for pets (e.g., 132" x 80")?

Yes. We build "Asset-Shield" protectors to any dimensions to preserve luxury mattress foam from pet accidents.

  • Stability Fit: We tailor the pocket depth (customizable up to 24"+) to ensure sidewalls stay locked during the lateral force of pets jumping on the bed.
  • 6-Sided Low-Lift Design: For RVs or tight spaces, our 6-sided encasements feature top-perimeter zippers so they can be installed without fully lifting heavy mattresses.

Dakimakura (Body Pillow) Modular System

Why do you recommend a 2-layer system instead of a single-layer sewn TPU unit?

Our Professional 2-Layer System is superior for three reasons:

  • Durability: Frequent washing can cause TPU membranes to delaminate if sewn directly to outer fabric. Our system lets you wash the outer cover (PremaCotton or BreezePlus) while the TPU liner remains protected.
  • Waterproof Integrity: Sewing TPU directly creates needle holes that can leak. A separate inner liner provides 100% spill-proof protection.
  • Acoustics & Feel: Separate layers ensure the pillow stays soft and quiet; bonding them creates a "crinkly" or stiff feel.
Duvet Covers & BedBridge Connector

What makes the 3-sided zipper better for heavy duvets?

The "Suitcase-Style" load allows the top layer to flip back completely. You simply lay the heavy insert flat and zip it closed, cutting bed-making time by ~90% and eliminating heavy lifting.
