How to Measure Your Mattress

The credit-card method for perfect accuracy — no tape measure required.

1

Grab a Standard Credit Card

Any standard credit, debit, or ID card works. Every card is exactly 85.60 mm (3.375 inches) long.

2

Measure Width (W)

Place cards end-to-end across the top of your mattress. Count full cards plus any remainder. Multiply by 85.6 mm. Example: 2 full cards = 171.2 mm.

3

Measure Length (L)

Repeat the same process from head to foot of the mattress. Record the total length in mm or inches.

4

Measure Depth / Pocket (D)

Measure from the top of the mattress down to where you want the sheet to tuck under. Use the same credit card method, or estimate: a card standing on its short edge is 53.98 mm (2.125 in).

Note: Every standard credit card is exactly 85.60 mm (3.375 in) long. This is an ISO/IEC 7810 ID-1 standard dimension used worldwide.
