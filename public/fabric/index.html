Our Fabrics

Four premium collections engineered for every lifestyle — from tropical cooling to marine durability.

BreezePlus Collection

Advanced Cooling Cotton Blend

A high-performance fusion of 50% Long-Staple Cotton and 50% Specialized Microfiber. Clinically shown to be 3-5°C cooler than standard cotton. It actively draws heat away from the body rather than trapping it.

  • Pet hair resistant surface
  • 100% chemical-free
  • Ideal for hot sleepers & tropical climates
  • Night-sweat & allergy friendly

Colors: 9 Cooling Colors (Dark Grey, Silver, Sand, Sky, Emerald, Sea, Pure White, Baby Pink, Ivory)

CloudSoft Collection

Premium Microfiber Excellence

Highly engineered 120 GSM double-brushed microfiber with a signature embossed pattern. Resists salt air and ambient humidity. Quick-drying and requires no ironing.

  • Moisture & salt-air resistant
  • Quick-drying, wrinkle-free
  • Soft & stain-resistant
  • Ideal for marine, yacht, and RV cabins

Colors: 12 Sophisticated Colors (Mint, Charcoal, Grey, Sapphire, Forest, Denim, RoseGold, Beige, Ovaltine, White, Lavender, Olive)

PremaCotton Collection

The Purest Natural Choice

100% Pure Long-Staple Natural Cotton. Becomes softer with every wash and offers the traditional “crisp” feel of luxury hotel linens. OEKO-TEX Standard 100 Certified.

  • OEKO-TEX Standard 100 Certified
  • Chemical-free purity
  • Ideal for sensitive skin & infants (0-6 months)
  • Luxury hotel crispness

EcoLuxe Collection

Natural Calico Comfort

Premium Calico (Greige) cotton in its natural, unbleached state. Woven in a durable plain-weave pattern that softens with every wash while maintaining its raw, earthy character.

  • Natural unbleached Calico / Greige cotton
  • Durable plain-weave that softens over time
  • Minimal processing — closer to nature
  • Ideal for eco-conscious families
