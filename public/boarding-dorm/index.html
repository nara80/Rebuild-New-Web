Bedding for Boarding Dorm — What Parents Need to Know

Every country has its own "single bed" definition. A US Twin is 39×75in, but a UK single is 90×190cm, and a Japanese single is 100×200cm. Your child might arrive at their dorm expecting their bedding to fit — only to find it doesn't.

MildMate solves this. We make single bed bedding in exact measurements, to fit the exact size your child needs wherever they are studying. And our 3-sided zipper duvet cover means laundry day takes minutes, not hours — just unzip, swap, zip up.
