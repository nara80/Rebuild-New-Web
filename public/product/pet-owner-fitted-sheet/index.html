Care Instructions

  • Machine wash cold (30°C / 86°F) — gentle cycle
  • Do not bleach — mild detergent only
  • Tumble dry low or hang dry
  • No ironing needed — wrinkle-resistant by design
  • Remove pet hair before washing — a quick vacuum or lint roll saves a rinse cycle

BreezePlus dries significantly faster than pure cotton — typically 2–3 hours on a line or low tumble dry. This also extends the fabric lifespan.
