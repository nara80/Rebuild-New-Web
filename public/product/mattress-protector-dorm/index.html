Care Instructions

  • Machine wash warm (40°C / 104°F) — gentle cycle
  • Do not bleach — mild detergent only
  • Tumble dry low or hang dry
  • Do not iron the TPU layer
  • Wash at least once per month to maintain hygiene

TPU is durable and maintains its waterproof properties through 100+ wash cycles at 60°C.
