Not Just "Pet-Friendly" — Actually Waterproof

Not just "pet-friendly" — actually waterproof. The TPU inner liner blocks 100% of liquid from reaching your mattress, while the outer BreezePlus layer keeps your pet comfortable. The full perimeter skirt with non-slip elastic keeps the protector locked in place even during the most active pet nights.

  • TPU waterproof inner liner — 100% fluid barrier
  • BreezePlus outer layer — breathable, comfortable for pets
  • Full-perimeter non-slip elastic — stays put on active beds
  • 5-sided fitted design — easy on/off for frequent washing
  • Custom skirt depth matches your mattress exactly

TPU + BreezePlus — Dual Protection Layer

The inner TPU (Thermoplastic Polyurethane) membrane is a thin but incredibly durable waterproof layer that blocks 100% of liquids — urine, vomit, spilled water — from reaching your mattress. Unlike PVC noisier waterproof layers, TPU is quiet, flexible, and certified safe for skin contact.

The outer BreezePlus layer adds comfort and breathability. Pets sleeping on the protector feel the cool, smooth BreezePlus surface rather than a plasticky membrane.

  • TPU inner — 100% waterproof, quiet, skin-safe
  • BreezePlus outer — breathable, pet hair resistant
  • OEKO-TEX certified — no toxic chemicals
  • Machine washable at 60°C

Care Instructions

  • Machine wash warm (40°C / 104°F) — gentle cycle
  • Do not bleach — mild detergent only
  • Tumble dry low or hang dry
  • Do not iron the TPU layer
  • Wash at least once per month to maintain hygiene

TPU is durable and maintains its waterproof properties through 100+ wash cycles at 60°C.
