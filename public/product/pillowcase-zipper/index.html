Zipper Pillowcase

Secure YKK zipper enclosure keeps the pillow perfectly in place. The zipper glides smoothly and stays hidden for a clean look. Custom-made to your exact pillow dimensions with PremaCotton option is OEKO-TEX® certified fabrics.

  • YKK zipper — smooth, durable, hidden design

Premium Fabrics

Available in 4 premium fabric options. CloudSoft for everyday luxury, BreezePlus for pet owners, PremaCotton for the premium feel, and EcoLuxe for natural unbleached cotton.

  • CloudSoft — 100% long-staple cotton, soft and durable
  • BreezePlus — Anti-fur weave, pet hair slides off
  • PremaCotton — Premium long-staple, extra silky feel
  • EcoLuxe — Natural unbleached cotton, earth-friendly

What fabric and care details should I know?
  • Close zipper before washing to prevent snagging
  • Machine wash warm (40°C / 104°F) — regular cycle
  • Do not bleach — mild detergent only
  • Tumble dry medium or hang dry
  • No ironing needed — wrinkle-resistant by design
