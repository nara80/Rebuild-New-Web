Lightweight Duvet Insert — Made in Thailand

Our Duvet Insert uses premium polyester hollow fiber filling that provides excellent warmth without the weight. Hypoallergenic and breathable — ideal for year-round comfort in Thailand's climate.

  • Polyester hollow fiber filling — hypoallergenic and dust-mite resistant
  • Lightweight yet warm — perfect for Thailand's climate
  • Available in 3 fill weights: Light (300g), Medium (400g), Warm (600g)
  • Standard Thailand sizes: 3.5ft, 6ft Queen, King
  • Custom sizes available — contact us for quote
  • OEKO-TEX certified filling
  • Quick-dry after washing

Product Details

  • Fill: 100% Polyester Hollow Fiber
  • Cover Fabric: Polyester (soft touch)
  • Fill Weights: Light (300g/m²), Medium (400g/m²), Warm (600g/m²)
  • Sizes: 3.5ft (107×191cm), 6ft Queen (120×200cm), King (152×198cm)
  • Custom Sizes: Available — contact us for pricing
  • Certification: OEKO-TEX certified
  • Country of Origin: Thailand

Care Instructions

  • Machine wash cold (30°C) — gentle cycle
  • Tumble dry low or hang dry
  • Do not bleach
  • Do not iron
  • Fluff up after washing to restore loft

The polyester hollow fiber filling dries quickly and maintains its fluffy texture wash after wash.
