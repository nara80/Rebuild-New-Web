Complete Waterproof Protection for Your Pillow

The Pillow Protector combines a TPU (Thermoplastic Polyurethane) waterproof inner layer with your choice of premium outer fabric. Blocks 100% of fluids — sweat, drool, pet accidents — while keeping your pillow clean and fresh. OEKO-TEX certified for safety.

  • TPU inner — 100% waterproof, quiet, skin-safe
  • 4 outer fabric options: CloudSoft, BreezePlus, CloudFlex, Bamboo
  • Envelop-style closure — fits all standard pillow sizes
  • OEKO-TEX certified — no toxic chemicals
  • Extends pillow lifespan by protecting from moisture and wear
  • Machine washable, quick dry

TPU + 4 Outer Fabric Options

TPU Inner: Thermoplastic Polyurethane membrane blocks 100% of liquids while remaining thin, quiet, and breathable. Unlike noisy vinyl, TPU feels natural and doesn't crack over time.

Choose your outer fabric:

  • CloudSoft — 400 thread count cotton feel, soft and OEKO-TEX certified
  • BreezePlus — Pet hair resistant, 50/50 cotton/microfiber blend
  • CloudFlex — 4-way stretch jersey, body-conforming comfort
  • Bamboo — Natural moisture-wicking, cooling, antibacterial

Care Instructions

  • Machine wash cold (30°C) — gentle cycle
  • Do not bleach
  • Tumble dry low or hang dry
  • Do not iron TPU layer
  • Remove promptly from dryer to prevent wrinkling
