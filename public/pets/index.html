Pet Owner Bedding — BreezePlus Engineering

Pets shed. That's a fact. But with BreezePlus fabric, pet hair slides right off the surface — no sticky rollers, no hours of vacuuming, no frustrating brushing sessions. BreezePlus is a synthetic micro-fiber engineered specifically for pet owners: smooth, dense, and inhospitable to fur entanglement.

  • Pet hair slides off — no sticky rollers or vacuuming marathons
  • 3-5°C cooler than cotton — better for pets and owners sharing a bed
  • Scratch-resistant weave — holds up to claws without pilling
  • Machine washable — dries in under 30 minutes on air dry
  • Odor resistant — stays fresher longer between washes
  • Available across fitted sheets, duvet covers, and pillowcases
